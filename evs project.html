1. GoodGuide

GoodGuide was an online web, iPhone app and Android app tool which enabled consumers to retrieve evaluations of the health, environmental, and social impacts of consumer products such as toys, food, and detergents 1. The app was designed to help consumers make purchasing decisions on the basis of the health, environmental and social impact of a product’s life cycle. The app provided tools for consumers to make purchasing decisions on the basis of the health, environmental and social impact of a product’s life cycle. These products were designed to provide consumers with this information right at the point of sale, to help them to apply their values to their purchases. The app read the bar codes on product labels using the phone’s camera, and provided a summary of the product’s score according to GoodGuide’s rating system

2. Plume Labs

Plume Labs is a company that provides solutions to help people reduce their exposure to air pollution. The company builds devices and apps that help measure personal exposure, plan journeys and activities, improve air quality in homes and workplaces, and persuade decision makers 1. One of the company’s products is Flow, a handheld device that measures air quality. The device is designed to answer the question “what is in the air I breathe?” 1. The device is no longer for sale, but the company has dedicated its knowledge, system, and technology to advancing research, raising awareness, and fostering action 1. The company also offers an app that provides air quality forecasts in your city. The app allows users to know what the day will be made of, plan ahead, and discover what the world is breathing. The app also provides air quality data and forecasts made available for businesses of all shapes and sizes 1. In addition to its products, Plume Labs also provides a blog that shares stories of change and ideas from the team. The blog provides insights into how the company is building a community and empowering people to take action. The company also offers a free Air Quality 101 course to help people learn more about air pollution and how to reduce their exposure 1.

3. TrashOut

TrashOut is an environmental project that aims to map all illegal dumps around the world and help citizens recycle more. The app offers a range of features to help users contribute to a cleaner world. Users can report any illegal dump they come across, and environmental organizations, municipalities, or active citizens in the area will be notified. Users can also choose to receive these notifications. The app also helps users dispose of their waste the right way. Users can easily find the right bin or collection center for their waste by filtering recycling bins and centers based on their size, type of waste they accept, and proximity to the user. Users can get directions to get there and see their opening hours. Anyone can add more bins and centers to help out 1

4. JouleBug

JouleBug is an employee engagement mobile app that educates and mobilizes people to take action toward their company’s sustainability goals. The app is designed to help companies achieve their sustainability goals by engaging their employees in real-world activities and hands-on experiences that shift habits and produce measurable results. JouleBug offers over 70 measurable sustainability actions that can be customized to align with a company’s values. 5. Eco-Connect

Eco-Connect a platform that links the Government, Corporate Executives, and Concerned Individuals to confront the difficulties brought on by global warming. : A company that aims to reconnect people with nature through services like ecological landscaping, biodiversity assessment, nature conservation, and environment education and awareness."EcoConnect" aims to reconnect people with nature through a wide array of services like Ecological Landscaping, Biodiversity Assessment, Nature Conservation and Environment Education and Awareness through a holistic approach. In today’s world, as we are developing more and more, we are moving further away from nature. Our development practices are human-centric with no regard and respect for nature and its original inhabitants. We are destroying the original terrain and displacing biodiversity due to a lack of knowledge towards the ecosystem and ecological processes. According to studies, people who have a close connection with nature are healthier, happier, and creative. It aids in their development as responsible and sensitive citizens. The connection of nature is linked to a greater pro-nature conservation behaviour that is crucial for future conservation efforts. ​EcoConnect is an attempt to bridge a gap between humans and Nature. ​“Study nature, Love nature, Stay close to nature, it will never fail you”. Our mission is to conserve and restore our natural resources, biodiversity and re-establish the co-existence of all living beings.
